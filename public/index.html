You will get more poitns per square as the game goes on.

Powerups:

+1000 points: adds 1000 points to your score
Invincibility: makes you invincible for 6 seconds
Slowmo: slows the game down for 6 seconds
Shrink: reduces the size of your square by 40%

Powerdowns:

-1000 points: subtracts 1000 points from your score
Evil Squares: all of the squares on screen are enemies for 6 seconds
Speed: speeds the game up for 6 seconds
Grow: increases the size of your square by 40%
